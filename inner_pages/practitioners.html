The Smart Way to Manage & Grow Your Practice

Booked is more than just a scheduling tool. It's your all-in-one platform to streamline operations, enhance client experience, and unlock your practice's full potential.

Too much time spent on phone calls and manual scheduling?

Our intelligent online calendar, website plugin, and automated reminders let clients book themselves 24/7. Plus, our unique AI Voice Booking can even handle calls for you from clients using the Booked app, even if the client's chosen practitioner isn't fully set up on Booked yet.

Losing revenue due to no-shows?

Drastically reduce no-shows with automated email, SMS, and voice call reminders. Secure commitments with optional pre-payment for services.

Struggling to attract new clients or retain existing ones?

Increase your visibility through the Booked client app. Offer client subscription packages for recurring revenue and loyalty.

Difficulty managing client information and communication?

Our integrated client management tools keep notes, history, and communications organized and secure.

Unsure how to optimize your schedule or pricing?

Leverage AI-powered demand forecasting to understand peak times and make data-driven decisions.
